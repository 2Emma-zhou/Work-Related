### Configuring on web

1. Log in to supLink web, and then click **App Manage**.
2. Under **LCDP App**, click **New**.
![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supLink/49.png)

<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Parameter</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Subscribe message</td>
<td>Enable it for the application to receive messages on mobile. Link a supOS app to receive notice from it.
 

:::info
Work with supOS **Notification Center**.
:::


</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Page config</td>
<td>Select whether to enable the page to switch from left to right, and rotate on mobile.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>List layout</td>
<td>Select the layout of displaying multiple pages and folders.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Visible range</td>
<td>Set the application visible range. Select roles to grant permissions when set <b>Visible range</b> to <b>Part visible</b> or <b>Part invisible</b>.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Select group</td>
<td>Select a app group for the application.</td>
</tr>

2. Click **Back** after saving the configuration, and then click **Setting** on the app.
3. Click **Setting**, and then select pages from the pop-up window to be part of the app.
