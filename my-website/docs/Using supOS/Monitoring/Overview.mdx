  <div>
    <p>
      In the operation center of supOS platform, you can monitor the running
      status of field equipment to realize easier operation and maintenance.
    </p>
    <p>The operation center offers following features:</p>
    <ul id="overview__ul_pfp_xx3_s5b">
      <li>
        Powerful monitoring : Provides operation information of all applications
        developed in the supOS design center, including overall system overview,
        running trend diagram and alarm list of each tag, and other rich,
        powerful graphics that reflect the field situation.
      </li>
      <li>
        Real-time dynamic display: Reflects real-time operation of field
        equipment. The real-time change and dynamic refresh of each object
        attribute is true and reliable, which greatly facilitates the monitoring
        and management of equipment.
      </li>
      <li>
        Vivid interface: Through the playback of process images with
        self-adaptive resolution, full-screen display, and manual drag-and-drop
        control, the monitoring and management of process images is more
        convenient and realistic, offering better operating experience.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      After logging in to the supOS platform, you might see two different
      desktops based on your configuration.
    </p>
  </div>
