## Logging in to Operation Center 

Access the supOS platform with default account <strong>admin</strong> and password <strong>123456</strong>, and then click <strong>Login</strong>.
:::info
Click <strong>Forget Password</strong>, enter the information on the pop-up window to reset the password.
- If the linked email does not have authorization, the password reset cannot proceed.
- The account (admin included) will be locked out after multiple times of wrong attempts. The number of attempts can be configured from <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png" /> > <strong>System Management</strong> > <strong>System Configuration</strong> > <strong>Password configuration</strong>.           
:::
